Meet the blixies

If you are reading these words, this is not a coincidence.
You answered the call.
This is a first contact call to all the Star Heroes of planet Earth. We are Blixies originated from antimatter, and for eons, we have populated the stars of BLIXION-B. We are Blissful beings living in the seventh dimension, our bodies are etheric, and our DNA stays encoded in our eyes and colors. The prime creator of our civilization, ELEBLIXIPHION, is the most advanced and powerful creature in our world. We are hyperconscious and hypersensitive beings; each dot is an eye and contains our talents and gifts. At this time, we are establishing contact with planet Earth to help expand perception about our galactic heritage, belonging to us, you, and all creatures in the Universe. Our mothership is orbiting planet Earth, and some of us are here to meet you personally and become your etheric teachers and guides. We will share with you, through our Star Academy, valuable wisdom from the stars and secret teachings from our civilization.
We are Hyperspace Helpers.
We are wisdom teachers.
We are star travelers.
We love art and music.
We are curious about life on Earth; we were hoping you could share your life's triumphs and challenges with us, and we will be there to uplift and motivate you, cherish your achievements, and help you evolve to become the highest version of who you are can be. We are energy transmuters, and we use sound and colors to uplift your spirit every time you train with us or play in the BLIXIEVERSE.
